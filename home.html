Introduction

Type in the name, type, or subtype of a pokemon into the search bar, the app will return all currently available cards in the Pokemon TCG game, and show their stats. Along with a link to purchase them!

Click on the "Favorite" icon to save the current card to the Favorites page for easy deckbuilding.

Go to the community page to see what cards others are using! Extremely helpful to get started on your own deck!
